Antoine De Paepe

LaTIM & UBO

I am a PhD student at LaTIM, under the supervision of Alexandre Bousse in the ACTION team. My doctoral research focuses on joint motion compensation and image reconstruction in low-dose contexts. I am deeply interested in inverse problems—including blind inverse problems—diffusion models, and plug-and-play methods. I can be reached at antoine.depaepe@univ-brest.fr.
